About

  • 2023-2025

    The Preparation Phase

    The journey began with a shared ambition: to revolutionize financial systems using artificial intelligence.
    In 2021, our core team committed to months of intensive research, training, and strategic planning. We explored the latest developments in AI, financial modeling, and regulatory environments. This phase laid the groundwork for everything to come — from understanding industry pain points to designing viable AI-driven solutions.

  • July 2025

    The First MVPs

    With a solid foundation in place, we started building.
    Our first MVPs included a market trend prediction engine and a risk analysis prototype for financial institutions. These early builds helped us validate our concepts, gather feedback from industry advisors, and prepare for real-world implementation.
    Though Investintech is not yet officially launched, these MVPs mark the beginning of our product journey.
